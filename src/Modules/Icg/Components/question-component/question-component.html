Does the patient have risk factors for antibiotic resistance?
Risk factors include:
  • Metronidazole use in the last three months
  • Macrolide (eg, clarithromycin, azithromycin, or erythromycin) use in the last three months
  • Residence in an area where H. pylori resistance to clarithromycin is ≥15% or where eradication rates with triple therapy are ≤85%
